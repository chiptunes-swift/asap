ASAP - Another Slight Atari Player
==================================

This page contains a pure-ActionScript player of Atari music.
You need http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/[Adobe Flash Player] installed and enabled in your browser.

+++
<object width="0" height="0" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" data="asap.swf" id="asap">
	<param name="movie" value="asap.swf" />
	<param name="FlashVars" value="file=X_Ray_2.sap" />
</object>
<input type="button" value="Stop" onclick="document.getElementById('asap').asapStop()" />
+++
